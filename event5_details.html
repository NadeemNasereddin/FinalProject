Art on the Quad

Description: Immerse yourself in creativity and inspiration at our Art on the Guilford Quad event! Join talented artists and enthusiasts for a day of artistic expression, live demonstrations, and interactive workshops. From painting and sculpture to photography and digital art, there's something for everyone to explore and enjoy.

Date: May 10, 2024

Time: 12:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Location: Guilford Quad, University Campus

Additional Info: All skill levels are welcome to participate. Bring your own art supplies or use the provided materials. Food and beverages will be available for purchase. Come unleash your creativity and be part of this vibrant celebration of art!
