Glitter and Glam

Description: Dive into a world of creativity and inspiration at our Glitter and Glam Event on the Guilford Quad! Join fellow artists and enthusiasts for a day filled with artistic expression, captivating demonstrations, and engaging workshops. Whether your passion lies in painting, sculpture, photography, or digital art, there's boundless opportunity for exploration and enjoyment.

Date: May 10, 2024

Time: 12:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Location: Guilford Quad, University Campus

Additional Info: Artists of all skill levels are encouraged to participate. Bring your own art supplies or take advantage of the materials provided. Indulge in delectable food and beverages available for purchase. Unleash your creative spirit and become part of this lively celebration of art!
